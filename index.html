Passionate About Creating

Hi there. My name is Rae

I'm a designer, a dreamer, a creator and a coder. I love donuts, coffee, and have an extreme dislike of pants. I began as a self-taught developer. In 2015, I earned my B.S. in Graphic Design and Illustration from Kentucky's Murray State University. In 2017, after many late nights and lots of coffee, I earned my Master's in Web Design and Development from the University of Denver. Not too shabby for a girl from a small Kentucky town.

From creating simple brochure sites, to custom Wordpress sites, and everything in between, I'm looking to give you a product you and your clients will love.

When I'm not dabbling in development and design, I'm illustrating or challenging myself with new graphic and web design layouts. I would say it was because I believe in practice makes perfect, but honestly, I just love what I do!
